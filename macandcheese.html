Healthier Homemade Mac and Cheese


To make this rich mac and cheese recipe healither we use whole wheat bread crumbs, low-fat milk and cheese, whole wheat noddles, and reduces the amount of butter. Serve with a salad for a healthy meatless dineer.


Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook time: 30 mins

Total Time: 50 mins

Serving: 4

Yield: 4 servings



Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package wole wheat macaroni (such as Smart Taste)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups shredded low-fat Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups low-fat (1%) milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 pinch paprika

Directions

Step 1

Perheat over to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cool elbow macaroni in boiling water, strring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 mintes. Drain.


Step 2

Melt 2 tablespooons butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir in flour to make a roux. Slowly add milk to roux, stirring constantly. Stir in Cheddar and Parlesan cheeses and cook over low heat until cheese is melted and sauce is thick, about 3 miutes. Place macaroni in large baking dish and pour sauce over macaroni. Stir well.
